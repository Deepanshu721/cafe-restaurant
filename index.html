About Us

About Us

Established in 2015, Dilli Brew House is an eclectic cafe adorned with industrial character, event-led evenings, and a warm dining atmosphere that mirrors the tone of the supplied references.

The experience blends cuisine, cocktails, live music, and relaxed social energy, making the space feel equally suited for casual meals, late-night conversations, and celebrations.

"Dilli Brew House in CP is the perfect blend of entertainment and cuisine, with live performances, a diverse menu, and a memorable rooftop mood."
Deepanshu Aggarwal Deepanshu Aggarwal
